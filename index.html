Our Noodlees'
Secret
...is a good pinch of salt and history.
父亲陈
(Fùqīn Chén, english: Pápa Chen)
started working on rice fields as a young boy. He always brought home some of his harvested rice and helped his mother making her very special rice noodles themselves. When she passed he kept on working on the rice fields and tought his children his mothers noodle recipe. Not too long the neighbours joined them for dinner almost every day. Chen decided to try himself out. He openend his very first restaurant in Guilin (桂林市) a small city in southern china. His success was not long in coming...

Our Noodlees'
Secret
...is a good pinch of salt and history.
李爸爸
(Lǐ bàba, english: Father Lee)
started working on rice fields as a young boy. He always brought home some of his harvested rice and helped his mother making her very special rice noodles themselves. When she passed he kept on working on the rice fields and tought his children his mothers noodle recipe. Not too long the neighbours joined them for dinner almost every day. Lee decided to try himself out. He openend his very first restaurant 1999 in Guilin (桂林市) a small city in southern china. His success was not long in coming...
今天
(Jīntiān, english: today)
Lee employs over 60 people in 5 restaurants across china. His children always by his side helped him expand the familiy restaurant to Brooklyn, NY in 2018 serving delicious rice noodle(e) dishes for every taste. All cooked after his mothers secret recipe. Come and say hi today!
